Update 19/02/2023 16:46 AEDT

Dear WebUrbanists community, We have some exciting news to share with you all. As part of our ongoing efforts to improve our Mastodon instance, we will be rebranding as masto.melbourne in three months.

With this change, we will also be moving away from our Urbanism-specific content and focus on becoming a more general regional instance. This means that we will expand our reach to include a broader range of topics and discussions related to the Melbourne area. We believe that this change will allow us to connect with an even wider community of like-minded individuals and foster even more meaningful conversations.

Our team has been working hard to ensure that the transition to Masto.Melbourne is as smooth as possible. We will continue to provide you with the same engaging and supportive space for artists, designers, and creatives to connect and share their work, but with a broader regional focus.

We understand that some of you may have questions or concerns about the change in focus. We welcome any feedback or suggestions you may have, and we encourage you to reach out to us with any comments you might have.

We hope that you will join us in this exciting new chapter for our Mastodon instance. We look forward to continuing to provide a vibrant and engaging space for the Melbourne community to come together and share their thoughts and ideas.

Thank you for your continued support of the WebUrbanists community.

Code of Conduct

The law of the land

WebUrbanists.com is committed to providing a positive and inclusive space for our users. We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or any kind of drama on our platform. We encourage our users to share their thoughts and ideas about urbanism, but posts do not have to be strictly about urbanism.

We welcome a variety of discussions and encourage respectful and constructive dialogue among our community. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates our content policy, including but not limited to:

  • Discrimination or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic
  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Spam or irrelevant content
  • Illegal or harmful activities
  • Pornography or sexually explicit content
  • We also reserve the right to suspend or terminate the accounts of users who violate our content policy.

We are dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all of our users, and we appreciate your support in upholding our content policy

While we wish we could, we cannot add every possible rule, and because of this we must mention that if we deem your behaviour or your actions not fit for this server, we have the right to punish.
